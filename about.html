About Us

Founded in 2021, FLAVA DOT GLOBAL is a local company with global ambitions.

with a collective 30 years of management and R&D experience, we bring a wealth of experience to the world of flavour production.

we are a flavour + seasonings manufacturer for the food and snack industry.
we specialise in creating BESPOKE and innovative Flavours for each and every one of our Customers. we take pride in our FLEXIBLE and RELIABLE production processes, ensuring that we meet the diverse needs of our Clients
At the heart of our business is personalized customer service, we believe in building strong relationships with our clients, understanding their unique requirements, and delivering exceptional results. To enhance safety and efficieny, we have implemented new food safety technology to provide our customers with the highest quality products.
our commitment to quality is evident in our compliance with FSSC 22000 V5.1 quality standards.
